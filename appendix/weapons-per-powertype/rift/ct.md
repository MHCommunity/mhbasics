### Weapon Acquisition
The Crystal Tower can be bought directly from the Trapsmith for 4,900,000 Gold and 1 Raw Rift Crystal in the Gnawnia Rift.
- A Raw Rift Crystal can be acquired by catching a Goliath Field Mouse.
